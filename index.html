Twitter GeoTool

Twitter GeoTool is a robust tool to help advertisers and others gain real-time information about what's #trending in a specific location, about a specific topic, anywhere in the world. The goal was to create a new reveue stream for the Twitter platform in lieu with the company's interest in a subscription based model.

LibertyBot

LibertyBot layers onto Liberty Mutual's existing mobile application. This interactive chatbot utilizes Natural Language Processing to assist customers with insurance policy needs and cultivates "individual intelligence" per customer. LibertyBot decreases customer wait time, builds customer query history, reduces administrative costs, and gathers powerful data.
